Catch the Point

How to Play

Offense Mode

  • You are the blue point, trying to capture the red point
  • Red will try to maintain distance and find escape routes

Defense Mode

  • You are the blue point, trying to evade the red point
  • Stay connected to multiple paths to avoid being trapped

Two Player Mode

  • Red uses WASD, Blue uses arrow keys, Blue is chasing red
  • Take turns trying to catch or escape each other

Game Basics

  • Each move removes one random edge
  • Win by either connecting points or completely separating them
  • Press Enter to restart anytime
